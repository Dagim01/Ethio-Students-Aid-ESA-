Here Are Some Advises

Below Lies Three Of The Things We Strongly Advise Students Of This Time Should Get Involved With. Our Belief Is That These Three Aspects, Tend To Improve The Human Mind Get The Better Of Themselves

Programming

Programming is arguably one of the most important skills for current as well as future generations to learn. For young learners, programming helps to gain problem-solving skills.

Ask Questions

Ironic coming from a Q&A website, but Asking questions is always progressing a step forward to ineviatable Success.

Video Games

The impact of Interest in Video Games has over the human brain shouldn't be underestimated. Apart from serving as a recreational material, Video Games also tend to improve problem solving skills.

About Us

We are a non-profit and non-governmental organization here solely for the purpose of helping young as well as adult ethiopian students through a means of providing help with answers to different types of questions raised by students. our other purpose here is to help students get informed about different kinds of opportunities that comes their way and make sure that they don't miss it.
